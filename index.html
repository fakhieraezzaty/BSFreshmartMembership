Frequently Asked Questions

What is BS Freshmart Member Card?

BS Freshmart Member Card is a loyalty programme that allows members to earn points, receive vouchers and enjoy selected promotions.

How do I earn points?

Present your member card or scan your member account during payment. Every RM1 spent earns 1 point.

Where can I view my voucher?

Open the BS Freshmart application and go to the voucher or rewards section.

How do I redeem my e-voucher?

Select an available voucher in the app and present the voucher code during checkout.

Can I use my points with promotion?

Points may be used with selected promotions unless the campaign terms and conditions state otherwise.

When will my points expire?

Accumulated membership points will expire after 2 years.
